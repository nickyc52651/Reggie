03/17/1980: Lucius, Regulus, and James talk in the Library

Summary:

Lucius is asking about how much contact Regulus and James had during Regulus' sixth year (he's avoiding asking Regulus if he remembers that James killed Pettigrew). James lists a few times they had contact, but they focus on The Named Visit Hogsmeade.

Lucius claims the purpose for the discussion is to help Regulus through the gaps in his memory without Kingley's questions triggering him. They discuss the Dagger Interview briefly. Lucius suggests telling the Aurors the truth--that Regulus was exhausted and barely surviving.

Regulus asks what he's going to tell the Aurors about Peter Pettigrew Dies. He recounts the fake story, because that's all he remembers, then rants about how awful Pettigrew was, including that he got the chance to be Sirius' brother and threw it away. Lucius tells him that that anger will make him sympathetic to the Aurors and the wizarding world at large.

Lucius informs Regulus that he'll be fine, advises him to take a calming draught before the second session of the Dagger Interview, and instructs him not to let go of James' hand until the interview starts.

Once Lucius leaves, James asks Regulus if he can hold him, and Regulus agrees. He tells Regulus that he's proud of how he handled Damasos visits Grimmauld Place, and they discuss Lestrange briefly. When James states that he doesn't like seeing Regulus hurt, Regulus turns into Pet.

Pet thinks James is a client and tries to teach him how to play with it. James becomes frustrated/sad/a bit unstable as a result, telling Remus that Regulus 'isn't [his] Reggie anymore'. Remus suggests using the Rousseau Surroundings Method to ground Pet. Pet activates the Audible Memory Amulet.

Remus asks James to activate his ring, but that doesn't ground Pet. This sends James spiraling. Remus focuses on James, leaving Pet to lay on the ground.

Frank finally arrives and tries to communicate with Pet using Lestrange Manor Touch Communication. It doesn't work, so Remus uses his ring to convince Pet that he's real and no Polyjuice Potion has been used. James does the same.

James lets Pet touch his hair to help ground it. Frank asks James how to prevent further spirals, and James responds that he doesn't know. Frank mentions that the Damasos visit seems to be wearing on James, and James reponds that racism from purebloods isn't new, citing Orion Black, Cassian Rosier, Cygnus Black, and Lucius Malfoy as examples.

Regulus, now acting as Lord Black, argues that it's his responsibility to protect James from racism when it happens in his presence. That leads to a discussion about consent, which triggers Pet briefly. James has Regulus walk him through the rings he's wearing to help ground him. Regulus mentions Rodolphus' ring, and when James mentions that Rodolphus was tracking him with it, Regulus breaks down.
