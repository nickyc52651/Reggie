12/29/1972: Rosier Gala

Summary:

Sirius has no visible marks, mostly because Regulus used a Healing Potion the night before. Regulus is suffering from some malnutrition, but considers it worthwhile.

The manor is decorated lavishly. There is a string quartet and fairy lights. The gala takes place in the ballroom. There were refreshment tables and cakes.

Barty Crouch, Sr. approaches the Black and insists on being referred to as 'Barty'.

Cassian approaches Orion and Walburga because Angelica is asking to have Bellatrix and Narcissa as bridesmaids for the Bloodworth Wedding and he wants to make sure that Druella Black is alright with that, but hasn't seen her or Cygnus Black yet.

Cassian greets Sirius and suggests Demetria Griffin and Veronica Snowbell as potential matches for them, which makes Sirius dissociate.

Narcissa helps Sirius out of their dissociation by giving them a cold drink to hold.

Sirius is 'officially' introduced to Evan. They're able to be pleasant to each other, and Barty says he thinks they'll get along well. They mention the Attack of the Bedsheets and Pettigrew Hair Incident.

Bellatrix talks to Regulus about tutoring him, then about being recruited by the Lestrange Pre-Death Eaters. She invites him to join.

Barty says something to Sirius while Regulus is gone, but before Sirius gets the chance to complain, Lord Mulciber spills wine on Patricia Rosier's dress. She asks Wulfric to help his father, then receives a nonverbal signal of disapproval from Walburga Black.

Barty claps Sirius on the back, making him flinch, which reveals the ongoing Grimmauld Place abuse to both Evan and Barty.
