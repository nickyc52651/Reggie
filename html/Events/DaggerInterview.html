March 1980 - Dagger Interview

Part 2 - March 17

Occurs in: SoS 161

Location: Grimmauld Place Parlor

Characters Present:

  • Lucius Malfoy
  • Kingsley Shacklebolt
  • Caleb Proudfoot
  • Alastor Moody
  • Frank Longbottom
  • Baldric Rousseau
  • Sirius
  • Remus
  • James

Summary:

Shacklebolt is asking the questions. He asks about the 1978 initiation ball. Regulus says that he told the Dark Lord why he didn't want Thomas Selwyn and William Snowbell on his team, and that Voldemort had asked about soul magic. He also admits that he lied about the Nameless process, claiming that the caster had to be related to the target by blood.

Regulus admits to manipulating Barty Crouch, Sr., and that doing so was his idea. He also admits to manipulating James, and states both that the Dark Lord wanted to break him, and that it was never going to work.

Regulus summarizes his impression of the other Named and briefly summarizes the alliance between Rodolphus and Fenrir.

Regulus explains how he trained his team. He admits to using Frank Corbins to get information on the Auror Department, along with Thomas. Shacklebolt asks if he enjoyed his experience, and Regulus gives mixed answers, finally admitting that he thought he would have more choice than he did.

Regulus summarizes his July 1977 letter to Dumbledore, admits that he told Dumbledore that his code name was Eagle because that was Thomas' code name and he was trying to protect him.

Regulus briefly talks about what happened with Stone, explaining that he was angry because he'd wanted to trust the Aurors.

Regulus admits that he lied to Dumbledore about being the only Death Eater at Hogwarts, states that he didn't believe Knowles was a danger to Hogwarts students, and that he didn't think Pettigrew would become a Death Eater.

Regulus talks about keeping Mulciber under control both for selfish reasons and strategic reasons, going on a bit of a rant about how much he hates Mulciber. He hesitates before admitting that Mulciber asked Rodolphus to make Pet look like him before torturing it.

Regulus yells at Lucius because he approved the Unforgivables Lesson Knowles gave.

Regulus has a flashback recalling when he saw the Unforgivables article. Shacklebolt suggests they stop for the day.

Once the Aurors have left the room, Regulus passes Lucius a note telling him that Regulus wants to publicly announce his identity as Pet. Lucius says he'll talk to Moody about it.

Frank comes into the room, interrupting the trio to ask them to come into the dining room, where Sirius, Remus, and James are waiting for them. James' injured arm is visible. The three Marauders announce that they're going to stay at the Sanctuary house for a day, promising to be back Wednesday morning. Regulus realizes that he's in love with James, even though he doesn't remember him. He also attempts to say goodbye to Siri, though this rapidly leads to a breakdown. Rousseau grounds Regulus by giving him orders to wait for him up in the library, suggesting that they could work on memory recovery while James is gone. Regulus agrees. Regulus promises to remember their first kiss by the time James comes back. Regulus calls him 'ridiculous'.
