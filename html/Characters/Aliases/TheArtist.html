The Artist

An alias for Bellatrix Lestrange in her function as part of The Named.

Known Facts:

  • Enjoyed using Fire Curses, especially the Ignis curse (SoS 160)

    • She would leave burns all over her victims, and The Merchant would take the victims' memories afterward to cover her tracks (SoS 160)

  • Enjoyed torture, tending to go too far with it (SoS 160)
