July 1977 Dumbledore Letter

Summary:

Per Regulus: I told him that there were eight Named. I told him all the names, but not who they were. I told him The Dagger's team was going to be fighting in the air and keeping an eye on Hogwarts. I told him I would be recruiting Hogwarts students. I told him the Death Eaters were manufacturing a rise in crime. I told him not to let the Order believe anything good about the Death Eaters because I was trying to warn him about Pettigrew
